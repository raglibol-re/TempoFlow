# 08 — Pitch

> Status: **draft** — finalize in Phase 5.

## Structure

1. **Problem.** The attention economy is backwards: platforms harvest attention, sell it
   to advertisers, pay you nothing; creators get crumbs.
2. **Reversal thesis.** FLOW makes money flow per second and visible: **out** to creators
   when you watch them, **in** from advertisers when you watch ads. Your ad-attention
   finances your creator feed — potentially self-sustaining.
3. **Live demo.** The money-flow moment (see [07-demo-script.md](07-demo-script.md)):
   out to creator, scroll-to-refund, then in from ads gated by real attention.
4. **Why Tempo (only-possible-here).** Thousands of sub-cent payments/sec to many
   recipients, instant settlement, deposit refunds — economically impossible where the
   per-tx fee exceeds the micro-amount. Tempo payment channels + MPP are the substrate.
5. **Market.** Creator economy + digital ad spend; a fairer rail for both sides; agentic
   commerce (machine-to-machine micropayments) as the wedge.
